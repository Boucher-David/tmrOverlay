# Car Radar Mocks

Generated preview screenshots for the car radar overlay.

- `car-radar-multiclass.png` shows close traffic, white/yellow/red proximity coloring, ring time-gap labels, and the early multiclass approach warning arc with live seconds gap.
- `car-radar-states.png` is a contact sheet covering clear/hidden, side pressure, multiclass approaching, and error reporting states.
- `states/clear-track.png` shows the correctly empty production-hidden state.
- `states/side-pressure.png` shows close same-class traffic on both sides.
- `states/multiclass-approaching.png` shows the early other-class warning scenario.
- `states/error-reporting.png` shows the visible fallback error state.
